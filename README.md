# 📊 Customer Retention & Churn Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing customer churn behavior using the Telco Customer Churn Dataset. The primary objective is to identify customer segments that are most likely to churn and provide actionable business recommendations to improve customer retention and maximize revenue growth.

Developed as part of the **Future Interns – Data Science & Analytics Internship Program**, this project demonstrates the use of business intelligence and data visualization techniques to solve real-world customer retention challenges.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Customer churn is one of the most critical challenges faced by subscription-based businesses and telecom companies. Losing customers not only impacts recurring revenue but also increases customer acquisition costs.

This analysis aims to answer the following business questions:

- Why are customers leaving the company?
- Which customer segments are most likely to churn?
- How do contract types affect customer retention?
- What impact do payment methods have on churn behavior?
- Which services contribute most to customer attrition?
- What strategies can help reduce customer churn?

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 📌 Overall Churn Performance
- Overall Customer Churn Rate: **26.54%**
- Total Customers: **7,043**
- Churned Customers: **1,869**
- Active Customers: **5,174**

### 📌 Customer Contract Analysis
- Customers with **Month-to-Month contracts** represent the highest churn segment.
- Customers enrolled in long-term contracts show significantly better retention rates.

### 📌 Internet Service Analysis
- **Fiber Optic customers** experience the highest churn rate.
- DSL customers demonstrate better retention behavior.

### 📌 Customer Demographics
- **Senior Citizens** have substantially higher churn rates compared to non-senior customers.
- Gender has minimal impact on customer churn behavior.

### 📌 Payment Method Analysis
- Customers using **Electronic Check** have the highest likelihood of churning.
- Automatic payment methods show stronger customer retention.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following strategies are recommended:

### 1. Promote Long-Term Contracts
Offer discounts and loyalty incentives to encourage customers to switch from month-to-month plans to annual subscriptions.

### 2. Improve Fiber Optic Customer Experience
Investigate service quality issues and enhance customer support for Fiber Optic subscribers.

### 3. Strengthen Senior Citizen Retention Programs
Develop personalized retention campaigns and dedicated support services for senior customers.

### 4. Encourage Automatic Payments
Provide incentives for customers to adopt automatic payment methods to reduce churn risk.

### 5. Implement Proactive Retention Campaigns
Identify high-risk customers early and engage them with targeted offers before they leave.
